Why Invest in Stone Field School

Assured Student Pipeline 100-150 confirmed students in Year 1 directly from Kidsverse School, with a guaranteed minimum 75 students every year post-Year 1 from the Kidsverse pipeline. Guaranteed Revenue Base Kidsverse Playschool and After School Rehan create a strong feeder ecosystem, supporting predictable student inflow and a more stable admissions funnel. Financial Support School transport is fully supported for the first 300 students, helping reduce early-stage pressure on the operating model. Risk Reduction Building loan and school transport EMI are covered till Phase 1, reducing investor financial burden during the launch and stabilization period. Established Brand Kidsverse is already a trusted education brand in the region, with a strong footprint across Fatehpur and Jawali backed by community reputation. Market Presence Active social media presence and community engagement are already driving a strong admissions funnel for future school growth. Prime Admission Setup SFIS admission office is located at After School Campus on the Rehan-Dhameta Highway, giving high visibility and easy access for parents. Growing Catchment The school serves a prime catchment zone with rising demand for quality, affordable, international-standard schooling close to home. Operational Experience Proven track record in running Kidsverse Playschool and after-school education systems gives SFIS a stronger launch foundation. Experienced Leadership Leadership brings 15+ years of corporate and education experience across TCS, HCL, UIDAI, and school operations. Faculty Launch Support Hiring support will be provided to ensure quality faculty onboarding and a smooth school launch. Future-Ready Vision SFIS is focused on 21st-century learning, AI and tech-enabled schooling, activity-based learning, leadership programs, and global exposure.
