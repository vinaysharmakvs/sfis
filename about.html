Founder-led vision

A school built by people who have worked closely with children and parents for years.

Stone Field is founded by Neha Sharma and Vinay Sharma, backed by partners from the USA and supported by educational thinking from India and abroad. The vision grows from the Kidsverse journey, where families trusted us with early childhood learning, confidence-building, communication, and activity-based education.

SFIS is the next step: a disciplined, ambitious, caring school where children can move from foundations to future skills with clarity.

Neha SharmaFounder, Kidsverse Education

Focused on child confidence, parent partnership, strong foundations, and nurturing learning environments.

Vinay SharmaTechnology and future-skills vision
