About Stone Field

Global vision. Local roots. Future-ready students.

Founded by Neha Sharma and Vinay Sharma, Stone Field International School is backed by partners from the USA and built to be the first international school of the area.

Founder vision

A school where ambition feels natural.

The founders imagined a campus where children do not have to leave the area to experience advanced labs, strong English communication, competitive exam preparation, sports, wellness, and confident grooming.

What we stand for

Discipline, exposure, confidence, and care.

Stone Field combines modern infrastructure with a student-first environment where staff members are dedicated, well-groomed, and focused on the whole child.

International Exposure

USA partner-backed vision with an openness to global learning habits and future opportunities.

Character and Grooming

Daily routines that build confidence, manners, discipline, communication, and presentation.

Local Accessibility
